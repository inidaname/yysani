A Profile in service and Activism

Engr. Yabagi Yusuf Sani is one of the leading lights in Nigerian Politics and Corporate Business World. Born on July 1, 1954 Yabagi Yusuf Sani attended East Primary School, Bida and Technical Secondary School, Kotangora, Niger State, Nigeria. He later proceeded to the Institute of Technology, New York, USA.

Yabagi is also a product of Colombia University, New York, USA where he studied Industrial and Management Engineering with emphasis in Operations Research. Yabagi also attended an Executive Management Program in Havard Business School in Boston Massachusetts.

Since his graduation and completion of the mandatory one - year National Youth Service (NYSC) in Nigeria, Engr. Yabagi has been a key player in Nigeria's Oil and Gas sector where he has helped the Government of Nigeria in the development of innovative schemes and strategies to block leakages in the ever porous sector.

For several years, he was the Head Consultant to the Federal Government on the Review of Export Guidelines for Oil and Gas and on the pre-qualification and appointment of Oil and Gas Export Pre-shipment Inspection Agents. He has also been a Consultant, working directly under Nigeria's Presidency in the following areas:

  • The Nigerian Extrative Industry Initiative (NEITI) Upstream and Downstream Hydrocarbon Measurement and Handling Study to determine amongst others the reliability of information and data produced by the industry.
  • NEITI Physical and Process Audit (Upstream and Downstream)
  • Study, Installation and commissioning of on-line stock-monitoring and control system for all NNPC/PPMC Depots across Nigeria.

Other Public Sector Engagements:

In Niger State, where Engr. Yabagi hails from, he has equally been involved in the search for better governance models for the benefit of the people. He has held the following positions:

  • Senior Special Adviser (Oil and Gas) to the Niger State Governor (2011 - 2012 )
  • Secretary, Committee on the development of Bida Petroleum Inland Basin
  • Director - General, Oil and Gas Development, Niger State.
  • Member, Board of Directors Advisory Council , EASCON (Sub-Committee on Public Private Partnership)
  • Yabagi also worked as the Depot Administrative Officer, ( NNPC - PPMC).

PRIVATE SECTOR ENGAGEMENTS:

In addition to his engagements in the public sector, Engr. Yabagi is also a private sector businessman where he controls great interests and enormous work-force as an employer of labour. He has held the following positions in the private sector:

  • Chairman, Board Establishment Committee of Intercity Bank Plc.
  • Chairman, Board/Management (IT Committee), Intercity Bank.
  • Chairman/Chief Executive, Telemetry Nigeria Limited.
  • Chairman/Chief Executive, Nigerco Nigeria Limited.
  • Managing Director, Fuel Vouchers Nigeria Limited.
INNOVATIONS/IDEAS INTRODUCED BY ENGR. YABAGI YUSUF SANI FOR SOLVING SOME OF NIGERIA'S SOCIO-ECONOMIC PROBLEMS:
  • Introduction of fuel vouchers for the first time in Nigeria. This was meant to eliminate the hazards of carrying cash by individual motorists and fleet operators,
  • Introduced the Two-tier system of petroleum subsidy in Nigeria. He conceived this to retain petroleum subsidies on fuel consumed by the productive sectors of the economy while the private sector continues to buy at the market price.
  • Development of a legal metrology infrastructure for verification of weighing and measuring instruments/equipment under the Weights and Measures ACT CAP W3 of 2004 Laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
  • Design and implementation of the on-line stock monitoring and control system for all the PPMC/NNPC Oil and Gas depots.
  • Design and implementation of a SCADA System for on-line Monitoring and Control of PHCN 33KV Feeders at all Power sub-stations all over Nigeria.
COMMUNITY SERVICE/SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY:

In his constant search for better ways to enhance the welfare of the Nigerian people, Engr. Yabagi Yusuf Sani carried out the following community-based initiatives/projects:

  • The establishment of the Nupe Foundation Cultural Organization to foster unity and socio-economic development among over ten (10) million Nigerians of the Nupe ethnic Nationality. Through his efforts, the Nupes in Nigeria now have an economic development community and several other programmes for economic, social and cultural integration.
  • Establishment of The North Central Unity Bridge (NCUBA a.k.a. THE BRIDGE) to promote socio-cultural integration, unity, political emancipation and economic development of the people in Nigeria's North Central region comprising: Niger, Kogi, Kwara, Plateau, Benue, Nasarawa states and the Federal Capital Territory (FCT).
  • Establishment of the Association of the Petroleum Inland Basins of the North (APBON) to fast track development of the hydro carbon deposits in the northern part of Nigeria.
  • As a mark of recognition for the formation of these Sociocultural, Political and Economic Development groups, the United Nations most distinguished Humanitarian Award for social development in Africa was conferred on him at a colorful ceremony on Thursday 13th September 2018 at the UN Headquarters in New York, USA.
POLITICS:

His exploits in Nigerian politics has been remarkable:

  • He is the founder of Nigeria's fastest growing political party – Action Democratic Party, ADP.
  • Niger state Governorship Candidate of the All Peoples Party (APP) in 1999.
  • Member, Board of Trustees (BoT) All Nigeria Peoples Party ( ANPP)
  • Governorship Aspirant in the United Nigeria Congress Party (UNCP).
  • National Financial Secretary, Liberal Convention ( LC).
  • Currently, the National Chairman and Presidential Candidate of the Action Democratic Party (ADP).
VISION FOR NIGERIA:

Engr. Yabagi Yusuf Sani is determined to chart a course for a New Direction in order to rebuild Nigeria into a safe and stable egalitarian democratic Nation with equal opportunities for all Nigerians irrespective of differences in ethnic, religious and political affiliations.

Presidential Ambition:

Engr. Sani is driven by the uncommon passion, zeal and patriotism to build a secure, United and prosperous Nigeria that is capable of taking its place as an important member of the International Community.

Strategies for re-positioning Nigeria:

Reforming the Regulatory Agencies for efficient and timely service delivery, enhanced security and Building global partnerships for development in:

  • Education
  • Health
  • Infrastructure
  • Economy
  • Mobilizing the abundant natural and human resources in Nigeria to build a virile Nation.
  • Develop new governance models that would usher in growth in GDP and overall economic development.
